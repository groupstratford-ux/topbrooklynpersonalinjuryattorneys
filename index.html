About STRATFORD LAW GROUP PLLC

STRATFORD LAW GROUP PLLC is a Brooklyn-based law firm specializing in personal injury and medical malpractice. Our attorneys are dedicated to getting maximum compensation for injured clients through aggressive negotiation and, when necessary, litigation.

We handle all types of injury cases — car accidents, slip & fall, workplace injuries, product liability, dog bites, and complex medical malpractice claims. We provide clear communication, thorough investigations, and personalized legal strategies.

  • Free case consultation — no upfront fees
  • Experienced trial attorneys
  • Compassionate client support
  • Proven track record of settlements & verdicts
